## A book to read more than once

A real feng shui book rewards a second reading. The first time, you follow the argument. The second time, with a pencil, you mark the one or two moves you could actually make in the home you actually have. Months later, a third reading shows you which moves stuck and which quietly reverted. The compass does not change. Your home, and the season you are in, do.
