## Voice rules for every block

- Combine `skills/writing-skill-hp/SKILL_WRITING_VOICE_HP.md` (prose rhythm, sensory specificity, scale comparisons, beat-level pacing) with `skills/shared/marketing/copywriting.md` (second-person address, clarity, no jargon).
- Honest framing every line. "Traditionally", "associated with", "the tradition reads", "supports the conditions for". Never "will give you", "guarantees", "produces".
- No em dashes anywhere. Use ` - ` or rephrase.
- Bold the shift word. Italics for emphasis. No exclamation points.
- Personalisation tokens: `{{firstName}}`, `{{kuaNumber}}`, `{{kuaGroup}}` (universal); `{{direction}}`, `{{directionShort}}`, `{{pinyin}}`, `{{gloss}}` (direction blocks only).
- Target word count per block: 2000-2500 (identity), 2000-2400 (good directions), 1200-1700 (bad directions), 800-1200 (closing).
- Each block opens with a sensory or narrative anchor in HP-voice register, then moves into clean copywriting register for the practical body, and closes with a forward-pointing line.
