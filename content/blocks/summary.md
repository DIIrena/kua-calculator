## A reminder on how to read a direction

- **For the bed:** the direction is the wall the headboard sits against, so your head points toward it as you sleep.
- **For a desk or seat:** the direction is what your body faces while seated. The chair, not the desk, is what the system reads.
- **For a door or a room:** the direction is what the door faces as it opens, or the direction the room broadly opens toward.
