# One move this week, {{firstName}}

You have read one focused reading, not the whole system. That is enough to act on. A single direction, with a clear place to put it, is all you need to test the idea in the home you actually have.

## Pick one move, then leave it

Choose the smallest change this reading pointed to. Just one. Make it, then set it down and live with it for seven days. Notice, without forcing the question, whether the room feels any different. If it does, keep it. If it does not, you have lost only the few minutes it took to turn a chair or shift a shelf.

A fixed compass sector means the same thing for everyone, but your directions are your own. So check the sector against your reading: if it falls on one of your four supportive directions, the move is doubly worth trying; if it is one to handle with care, make the change gently and watch how it sits.
