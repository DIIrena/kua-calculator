# Anchor pages for promotion - selection

**Date:** 2026-06-08
**Scope:** myfengshuihome.com (whole site, not only the planner)
**Status:** Approved by owner. Use as ground truth for the channels and content-calendar phases that follow.

---

## What this document does

Picks the 5 guide pages that get the first promotional investment, the 5 secondary pages that get cross-linked and pushed next, and lists every other page that stays out of the promotion rotation for now.

The selection is the result of a 7-dimension scoring pass (loader -> 7 parallel critics -> synthesizer -> adversarial reviewer) over all 38 published guide pages on 2026-06-08. Each page was scored 1 to 5 on:

1. SEO and Google traffic potential
2. Pinterest and shareability potential
3. Fit with the free Kua calculator
4. Fit with the 2026 Annual Feng Shui Planner waitlist
5. Reader usefulness for beginners
6. Internal linking strength
7. Commercial value without sounding salesy

Two sanity gates were applied to the top picks: (a) the page must not be gated (cold traffic cannot reach a sign-in wall), (b) the page must score at least 4 on SEO or 4 on Pinterest (no anchor without an entry channel).

The owner then approved a swap: `annual-cures-vs-permanent-fixes` enters the top 5 in place of `five-element-cures-room-by-room`, so the planner waitlist gets at least one anchor of its own. Reasoning: the anchor set should mirror the funnel shape, mostly Kua calculator entries with one clear Planner entry. The Planner funnel needed a top-tier page or it would have been served only by cross-links.

## Top 5 anchors

### 1. Find your Kua number and your four directions
- **Route:** `/guide/compass-school/find-your-kua-number`
- **Cluster:** compass-school
- **Total score:** 29 / 35
- **Per dimension:** SEO 5, Pinterest 4, Kua-fit 5, planner-fit 1, beginner 5, linking 4, commercial 5
- **CTA:** Kua calculator
- **CTA text:** `Get your Kua number with the calculator`
- **CTA URL:** `/kua-calculator`
- **Content angle:** Your Kua number sorts you into East or West group and gives you four supportive directions. Run the numbers, then look at where your bed already points.
- **Why it anchors:** The only page scoring 4 or 5 on every promotion dimension. Search intent is high, the calculator is the literal tool the page describes, the four-directions framing designs as a clean Pinterest infographic, and the page also feeds inbound links into the rest of the Compass School cluster.

### 2. The southeast wealth area and how to read it
- **Route:** `/guide/money/the-southeast-wealth-area-and-how-to-read-it`
- **Cluster:** money
- **Total score:** 25 / 35
- **Per dimension:** SEO 5, Pinterest 4, Kua-fit 1, planner-fit 2, beginner 5, linking 3, commercial 5
- **CTA:** Kua calculator
- **CTA text:** `See how the southeast lines up with your favourable directions`
- **CTA URL:** `/kua-calculator`
- **Content angle:** The wealth corner is a surface, not a shrine. Walk to your southeast, look at what is actually there, and pick one act of care for the week.
- **Why it anchors:** Wealth-corner queries are one of the highest-intent beginner searches in feng shui. The page handles it without manifestation language so it can rank without the usual disclaimer drag. The six-lever framing makes the corner readable instead of mystical, and the page sits naturally next to the calculator for readers who want to extend the read to their own directions.

### 3. Bedroom, kitchen, and front door
- **Route:** `/guide/rooms/bedroom-kitchen-front-door`
- **Cluster:** rooms
- **Total score:** 25 / 35
- **Per dimension:** SEO 3, Pinterest 5, Kua-fit 3, planner-fit 1, beginner 5, linking 4, commercial 4
- **CTA:** Kua calculator
- **CTA text:** `Find the direction your bed and door should favour`
- **CTA URL:** `/kua-calculator`
- **Content angle:** The three rooms that compound hardest. Where the bed sits, where the stove sits, what the door opens onto. Layout first, then direction.
- **Why it anchors:** The holy trinity of beginner feng shui rooms in a single page. Pinterest performance is maximal because bedroom and kitchen layouts are evergreen aesthetic categories. The bundling that hurts SEO is exactly what helps it on social, and the bed and door layout naturally invite the calculator as a deeper read.

### 4. Annual cures vs permanent fixes (the planner anchor)
- **Route:** `/guide/cures/annual-cures-vs-permanent-fixes`
- **Cluster:** cures
- **Total score:** 22 / 35
- **Per dimension:** SEO 4, Pinterest 2, Kua-fit 1, planner-fit 5, beginner 2, linking 3, commercial 5
- **CTA:** Planner waitlist
- **CTA text:** `See the 2026 Annual Feng Shui Planner`
- **CTA URL:** `/products/annual-feng-shui-planner-2026`
- **Content angle:** Annual cures move at Li Chun, not at New Year's Eve. The 5 Yellow and 2 Black reset each year; the permanent fixes do not. Two timing horizons, one home, one calendar.
- **Why it anchors:** This is the only top-tier page that genuinely services the planner channel. Planner-fit is 5 because annual feng shui timing is exactly what the planner is for. Commercial fit is 5 because the page leads naturally into a calendar product without forcing a CTA. The trade-off is lower Pinterest (2) and beginner usefulness (2) because the topic is intermediate; we accept that in order to serve the planner channel at the anchor tier.

### 5. How to read any room
- **Route:** `/guide/rooms/how-to-read-any-room`
- **Cluster:** rooms
- **Total score:** 24 / 35
- **Per dimension:** SEO 3, Pinterest 4, Kua-fit 2, planner-fit 1, beginner 5, linking 5, commercial 4
- **CTA:** Kua calculator
- **CTA text:** `Add the direction lens to your walk`
- **CTA URL:** `/kua-calculator`
- **Content angle:** A reading is a walk, not a verdict. Four things to read in each room, in the order qi walks the home. Notes first, lenses after.
- **Why it anchors:** The walkthrough method is the page every other room and cure page points back to. Beginner usefulness is maximal because it answers the literal first question (how do I look at my place), and the four-thing checklist designs as a clean Pinterest pin. Pairs well with the calculator as a companion read when the direction lens enters the walk.

## Defer list (28 pages, out of promotion rotation for now)

Grouped by reason:

### Gated or terminal reference (no cold-traffic conversion)
- All 4 glossary pages: `glossary-core-feng-shui-terms`, `glossary-schools-directions-and-bagua`, `glossary-cures-rooms-and-elements`, `glossary-timing-and-sister-disciplines`
- Reason: Pinterest 1, commercial value 1; pure reference, converts no one even when it ranks.

### Hubs but not entrances (high inbound links, low promotion fit)
- `flying-stars-btb-and-timing` (planner-fit 5 but Pinterest 1, beginner 1)
- `what-the-bagua-is` (linking 5, beginner 5, but Pinterest 2, commercial 2)
- `what-feng-shui-is-plainly` (beginner 5, linking 5, but commercial 1)
- `five-elements-as-a-design-language` (linking 5, but planner-fit 1, Kua-fit 1)

These are destinations, not entrances. They earn their links by being good landing places, not by attracting cold search or social traffic.

### Expert-only (intermediate-to-advanced vocabulary)
- `qi-men-dun-jia-date-selection-and-strategy` (beginner 1)
- `bazi-birth-data-and-personal-timing` (beginner 1)
- `what-belongs-to-feng-shui-and-what-sits-beside-it` (beginner 2, meta-taxonomy)

Promote only after an intermediate audience builds; not entry pages.

### Bundled topics hurting SEO
- `bathrooms-storage-problem-rooms`
- `bedroom-kitchen-and-bathroom-health-check`
- `air-light-damp-and-stale-corners`
- `plants-materials-cleanliness-and-daily-rhythm`
- `healthy-home-feng-shui-without-health-promises`

Title bundles three topics each, which dilutes query match. Useful supporters once the room cluster is warm.

### Other defer (mid-tier scores, no standout dimension)
- `what-is-compass-school`, `three-orientations`, `compass-vs-front-door-methods`, `what-feng-shui-cures-actually-are`, `four-feng-shui-schools-in-one-map`, `form-school-vs-compass-school`, `reading-a-room-in-element-terms`, `the-productive-and-controlling-cycles`, `mirrors-plants-light-sound-and-symbols`, `living-dining-home-office`, `missing-corners-and-irregular-homes`, `crystals-symbols-and-optional-tools`

Some are strong on one dimension (Pinterest, SEO, or planner-fit) but lack a complementary second dimension to anchor a promotion arc. They get cross-link traffic from the anchors but no direct push.

## What is in scope and what is out

**In scope for the next phase (channels):**
- For each of the 5 anchors, pick the channels that fit (Google SEO investment, Pinterest pin shape, Reddit / forum thread, email)

**Out of scope, deliberately:**
- Payment work (Stripe, Lemon Squeezy)
- Consultations or service offers of any kind
- Rewriting any guide page content
- A content calendar (that comes after channels)
- Paid ads (that decision comes only after organic channels show signal)
