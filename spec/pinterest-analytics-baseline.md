analytics, so "did it work" is read from three free sources:

2. Vercel Web Analytics pageviews on /kua-calculator and the guide
3. Supabase row counts: product_waitlist (demand) and product_orders (sales)

## What to watch, and when

- A brand-new Pinterest account distributes slowly. Week 1 is seeding,
  not harvesting. Do not read low early numbers as failure.
- Check the pin's stats in Pinterest after ~7 days: impressions tell
  us if Pinterest is showing it; outbound clicks tell us if the image
  + title earn the click.
- Watch Vercel pageviews on /kua-calculator for any lift correlated
  with pin activity.
- First real signal of product-market fit: a waitlist signup that is
  NOT the owner, or a calculator session from Pinterest traffic.

## Decision triggers (from the plan)

- Combined email list crosses ~150: turn on the 3-email welcome
  sequence.
- Waitlist -> purchase on a launch email under 3%: price-test before
  concluding demand failure.
- Pin outbound clicks flat after week 4: shift pin targets back to
  guide pages and let the in-guide CTAs convert.
