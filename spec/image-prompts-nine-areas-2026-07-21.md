# Nano Banana Pro prompts - Nine Life Areas photo plates (2026-07-21)

Twelve photoreal interior plates for the premium Nine Life Areas Compass.
Owner generates and reviews each; approved files go into `content/photos/`
named exactly as headed below (see the README there for export settings:
JPEG, quality ~70-80, longest edge ~1600px, <= 250KB each, <= 2.5MB total).

Shared art direction, already written into every prompt: editorial interior
photography for a calm premium home magazine; ivory and warm-white walls;
natural daylight; matte natural materials (wood, linen, ceramic, brass);
restrained accents of deep pool-table green and warm terracotta; quiet
luxury, eclectic minimalism, a well-kept room. No people, no readable text,
anywhere, ever. If a generation shows lettering, hands, or a face in a
frame, regenerate.

Aspect ratios: cover is portrait 4:5; all others landscape 3:2. Set the
ratio in the generator where possible; otherwise generate large and crop.
